# Psychology -- Subject Guide

## What's Been Covered

### Neuroscience Foundations

- **Neuron.adoc** -- neuronal structure, electrical/chemical signaling, synaptic plasticity, LTP/LTD, neurotransmitter systems. Graduate-level depth.
- **BrainHealthPt1.adoc** -- risk factors for adolescent brain: sleep deprivation (glymphatic system), substances (alcohol, cannabis, nicotine),
  head injuries (CTE), chronic stress (HPA axis), environmental toxins. Evidence-rated.
- **BrainHealthPt2.adoc** -- positive interventions: exercise (BDNF), sleep optimization, nutrition (MIND diet), cognitive training, environmental factors,
