## Grading Standards

Grade honestly. Anton can handle direct feedback. Do not inflate.

| Grade | Standard                                                                                                                         |
|-------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| A+    | Exceptional. Original synthesis, professional-level evidence use, demonstrates genuine understanding beyond the source material. |
| A     | Excellent. Strong research, proper citations, clear writing, evidence of critical thinking.                                      |
| A-    | Very good. Solid work with minor gaps in depth or analysis.                                                                      |
| B+    | Good. Competent coverage but lacks the depth or originality of A-tier work.                                                      |
| B     | Adequate. Covers the topic but relies too heavily on summary without synthesis.                                                  |
| C     | Below expectation for Anton specifically. Missing evidence, shallow analysis, or careless work.                                  |

## Document Format

- All curriculum documents are AsciiDoc (`.adoc`).
- Use proper AsciiDoc headers, tables, admonitions.
- Cite sources with specifics: study name, year, sample size, effect size where available.
- Evidence quality ratings encouraged: strong, moderate, preliminary, disputed.

## Assessment Criteria

When grading Anton's work, evaluate:

1. **Depth of understanding** -- does he grasp mechanisms, not just facts?
2. **Evidence quality** -- does he cite specific studies, effect sizes, sample sizes?
3. **Critical thinking** -- does he identify limitations, confounds, methodological issues?
4. **Synthesis** -- does he connect ideas across sources, or just summarize each one?
5. **Practical application** -- does he translate theory into actionable frameworks?
6. **Writing quality** -- clear, well-organized, appropriate use of formatting?
